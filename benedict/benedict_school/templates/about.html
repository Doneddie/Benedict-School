About Us

Our Mission and Vision

To contribute to the enhancement of education and morals through holistic education. Our values are rooted in competent, confident, and morally upright persons.

To be the best Early Childhood Development (ECD) center in the area and produce competent and confident God-fearing persons.

Slogan

Heads not Tails. Deuteronomy 28:13

History and Founding

Benedict Kindergarten and Primary School was founded in 2015 by a group of dedicated educators passionate about early childhood education.
Our vision bearer is Mr. Ssebowa Francis and the school started as a family project in memory of our late father Benedict Mukasa (RIP).
It was mainly built to give back to the community where our late father lived and to keep his legacy alive.
The school is mainly run by two directors, Julian Nalwoga Sewanyana and Rose Makumbi, the head of administration and director of finance respectively.
Over the years, we've grown into a vibrant community committed to the holistic development of our children.
