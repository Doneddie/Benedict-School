About Us

Our Mission & Vision

At Benedict Kindergarten & Primary School, we aim to provide a nurturing environment where every child can thrive academically and socially. Our values are rooted in integrity, respect, and a passion for lifelong learning.

We envision a community where education is a collaborative journey that fosters creativity and critical thinking.

History & Founding

Benedict Kindergarten & Primary School was founded in 2005 by a group of dedicated educators passionate about early childhood education. Over the years, we've grown into a vibrant community committed to the holistic development of our students.
