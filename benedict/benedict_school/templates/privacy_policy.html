Safety First: Child Protection Policy

At Benedict Kindergarten and Primary School, the safety and protection of children is our top priority. This Child Protection Policy outlines our commitment to ensuring the physical, emotional, and social well-being of every child under our care.

Our Commitment to Child Protection

  • We ensure that all staff undergo comprehensive background checks.
  • We create a safe, supportive environment that encourages children to express their needs and concerns.
  • We provide staff training on identifying and responding to child abuse or neglect.
  • We have clear procedures in place for reporting suspected abuse or neglect.
  • We work closely with local authorities to maintain the highest standards of child protection.

How We Ensure Child Safety

  • Supervision: Children are never left unsupervised, especially in potentially vulnerable situations.
  • Secure Environment: Our facilities are regularly inspected for safety, and we have secure access protocols in place.
  • Child-Friendly Approach: We create an environment where children feel safe to voice their concerns, and their privacy is respected.
