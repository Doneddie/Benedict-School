Admissions Overview

Thank you for considering Benedict Kindergarten and Primary School for your child’s education. Here's what you need to know:

Key Dates

  • Applications for the 2024-2025 school year are now open.
  • Early registration ends on 15th January, 2025.

How to Apply

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (proof of previous education, etc.).
  3. Schedule a tour of the school.
  4. Attend an interview or orientation (if applicable).
  5. Receive admission confirmation.
Apply Now

Requirements

Children must be between the ages of 3 and 5 to apply for Kindergarten or 6 and 10 for Primary.

Documents Needed

  • Birth certificate
  • Proof of previous education
  • Medical records
  • Parent identification
